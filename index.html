How it runs

Event Structure

01Opening Ceremony — Kick-off, welcome addresses & problem statement release
02Development Phase — Continuous 24-hour build sprint with full mentor access
03Mentor Sessions — Scheduled technical guidance from Brototype experts
04Final Submission — Code freeze and project packaging
05Demo & Evaluation — Pitching to judge panel; live demonstrations
06Prize Distribution — Awards, recognition & closing celebration
What we need

Requirements from College

📶

Internet & Network

  • Dedicated leased line — minimum 500 Mbps (recommended 1 Gbps)
  • Support for 1,200–1,500 concurrent devices
  • Full campus WiFi with multiple access points
  • Backup internet (secondary ISP / 5G failover)
  • Enterprise-grade routers & switches
  • UPS backup for all networking systems
🔌

Power & Electrical

  • 24-hour uninterrupted power supply
  • Generator / inverter backup
  • Minimum 1 socket per participant
  • Heavy-duty extension boards
  • Dedicated circuits for participant, stage & networking areas
🏫

Venue & Space

  • Capacity for 500+ participants
  • Large hall(s) or multiple classrooms
  • Dedicated coding, mentor, rest & dining zones
  • Stage with projector / LED display
  • Registration & coordination desk
🛏️

Overnight Stay

  • Official overnight institutional permission
  • Separate rest areas for boys and girls
  • Mats / chairs for rest
  • 24-hour washroom access
  • Continuous security monitoring
🍽️

Food & Refreshments

  • Dinner & midnight snacks
  • Breakfast for all participants
  • Tea / coffee stations throughout
  • Continuous drinking water availability
  • Organized distribution system
🔐

Security & Permissions

  • Institutional approval for event
  • 24-hour campus security staff
  • ID-based entry system
  • Controlled access points
  • Emergency response readiness
🎤

Event Infrastructure

  • Stage with full sound system
  • Projector / LED display screen
  • Campus-wide announcement system
  • Helpdesk & coordination desks
  • Event management support system
🧯

Safety & Emergency

  • First aid kits on-site
  • On-call medical support
  • Fire safety compliance
  • Clearly marked emergency exits
🧑‍💻

Operations & Volunteers

  • 20–30 volunteers required
  • Teams: Registration, Tech Support, Crowd, Food, Stage
  • Cleanliness & waste management
